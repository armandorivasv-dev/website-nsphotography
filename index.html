ABOUT ME

NICO SANDER

DUBAI

Hello my name is Nico, the world is my home, I am a frequent traveler who enjoys the ride with my lovely wife, my amazing son and my beloved dogs.

I live the present, embrace its magic, allow myself to feel it, knowing at the same time that it slips through my hands. I grab my camera and freeze that piece of time, turning the common into something particular, unique and exclusive.

There cannot be a day without a night, thanks to what is real we have an ideal. There is a gap between what it should be and what it can be, and in the middle, I find my playground, that fascinating place where I create, where the time is only an illusion, where everything is possible, giving my art to the world.
